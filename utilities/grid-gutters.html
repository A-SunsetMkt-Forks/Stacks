Gutters

Grids, by default, have no gutters between cells. When gutters are desired between cells, you can apply to them universally to grid items, along the x-axis, or the y-axis. Our gutters follow our spacing system values (2px, 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, and 64px).

To apply gutters to your grid, use the .gs[X], .gsr[X], or .gsc[X] classes. These apply gutters universally, along the x-axis, or the y-axis, respectively. Note: the spacing unit in the class name (i.e. gs4) is the desired total gutter space between each cell, not the space added to each cell.
