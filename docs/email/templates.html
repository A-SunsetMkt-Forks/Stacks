Short Stack

Good for simple emails with an “FYI” type of message.

  • Forgot Password
  • You’ve been removed from a Team
  • Take our Survey

Tall Stack

Good for emails with an “It’s important you acknowledge or address this” type of message.

  • Your answer was accepted
  • You’ve been invited to a Team
  • A new featured job you might be interested in

Full Stack

Good for emails with complex or multiple messages.

  • Tag digests and newsletters
  • Product updates and announcement
  • New recommended candidates
